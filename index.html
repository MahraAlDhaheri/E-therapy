Introducing Care Rooms

We set up chat rooms where individuals can anonymously express their concerns to others going through similar issues. After examining each member's issues and assigning them to the appropriate current groups or new groups, these rooms will be formed. There will be designated spaces where those with concerns can speak with a licensed therapist on duty around-the-clock. The purpose of these groups is to provide a safe space where people can discuss their concerns without worrying about being judged, in the hopes of either finding a solution or at the very least discovering others who are dealing with similar challenges. With both members' permission, there would also be a provision for one-on-one conversations.

3-Stage Helping Skills Model

A three-stage model for assisting clients was developed by Dr. Clara E. Hill. This method emphasizes the importance of a variety of ideas, such as empathy, working in tandem with the client throughout therapy, taking cultural factors into account, and putting the client's needs first.

1st Stage

Exploration
  • Involves assisting the client in reflecting on
    his or her feelings and thoughts. In order to
    assess and diagnose the patient's condition,
    we used AI-based technology, thus it is crucial
    to work closely with the client during the entire
    model. Attending, watching, and listening are
    the three main objectives of the exploration
    stage, along with assisting the clients in
    exploring their feelings and thoughts.

2nd Stage

Insight
  • Aids clients in understanding the causes of
    these sensations and thoughts. and to help them
    comprehend their issues more deeply. this stage
    denotes developing fresh perspectives, new
    insights, or new connections. Once clients have a
    basic awareness of their issues, they frequently
    move on to consider what they would like to
    change about their life.


3rd Stage

Action
  • Entails the customer changing. In this phase,
    the goal is to assist clients in determining
    if they want to alter their lives or not. The
    platform will work with clients to decide what
    adjustments to make, how to go about making
    those changes, and how to revise action plans
    when the inevitable barriers come up if clients
    decide after weighing the benefits and drawbacks
    of changing that they do, in fact, want
    to change.
